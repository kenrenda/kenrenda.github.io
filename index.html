About

Hello there! My name is Ken Renda or Ken Nyirenda, if you prefer my real name, but I like the sound of the first one better, so that's what I go by. I'm originally from Malawi in Africa, where I was born and raised in the city of Lilongwe. I've only ventured out briefly for school and other adventures, but I always find myself drawn back home.

In 2020, I decided to embark on a journey in graphic design. It was a decision to do something tangible and it's been a blast ever since. Then in 2022, I expanded my skillset and delved into web development. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology at the Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences (MUBAS), which I enrolled in 2020.

You must be here because you're interested in my work. Well, you're in luck. This website was created for people like you, so feel free to browse through. If you like what you see and have something we should work on, don't hesitate to contact me. Let's make something great together.

My Work

My work revolves around creating and offering tailored solutions to individuals and businesses as a web designer and developer, as well as a graphic designer. I am able to offer a wide range of services to meet the specific needs of my clients whether it's designing a new website or creating a new brand identity. I am committed to delivering high-quality work that exceeds expectations. I pride myself on being able to understand my client's unique needs and goals and using my skills to create solutions that align with their specific requirements.

Graphic Design

In my field of graphic design, I specialize in print design, brand identity, product packaging, and user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design. I have a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating visually pleasing designs that effectively communicate the message. I understand the importance of creating a strong brand image and strive to create logos, typography, and color schemes that stand out and leave a lasting impression. With my packaging design experience, I know how to create designs that are not only eye-catching but also functional, helping businesses to increase the sales of their products. And on top of that, user experience and user interface design is my specialty, I design interfaces that are easy to use, visually appealing and help to deliver the desired message.

I believe that design can be powerful, and my aim is to always make my clients satisfied, their happiness is what drives me to work harder and create designs that surpass their expectations. I will explain more about my services in graphic design in the dedicated page, where you'll also be able to see my work and get a better idea of what I'm able to do.

Web Developing

In web design and development, I am a full stack developer, which means I have experience and knowledge in both front-end and back-end development. I take a holistic approach to web design, starting from the design phase, through the development process, and all the way to the publishing stage. I help businesses and individuals create professional and user-friendly websites that not only look great but also function smoothly and effectively. I work with various technologies to build responsive and dynamic websites that are optimized for search engines and provide a seamless experience for the users. Whether you need a simple brochure website or a complex web application. I have the skills and expertise to bring your project to life. The website you are currently on is one of my creations.
