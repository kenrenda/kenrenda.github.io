About Me

I grew up in Lilongwe, Malawi. The first born in a family of two children. At home it was mostly the four of us. Most people say it must be lonely when they hear that. But being around such a few people and alone sometimes kind of made me get used to it, hence am not that out going. Invite me to a party I'll most definitely find an excuse not to show up.

On my professinal side, I embarked on my graphic designing journery in the year 2020. That was after I had finished my secondary school education. It has now been over two years since I started graphic designing, working as a freelancer. My main focus being on logo design, flyer design, T-Shirt design and other designs I will talk about in the graphic design section lest I exhaust everything here.

In the same year 2020, I got enrolled at the Malawi University of Business and Applied Sciences (MUBAS), to study Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. On top of basic IT skills, am into software application development and web development. This website being one of my products.

If you have a project idea in mind, be it graphic design related or you need a website or a software application developed, feel free to contact me. I will be happy to hear from you.
